Record Tourism Numbers in 2023

Published: January 15, 2024 | Category: Visitor Statistics

India welcomed over 10 million foreign tourists last year, with historical sites being the top attractions.

Key Highlights

  • Taj Mahal saw 7.5 million visitors
  • 30% increase from 2022 numbers
  • USA and UK top source countries
