The Spiritual Heart of India

Varanasi, also known as Benares or Kashi, is one of the world's oldest continually inhabited cities and the spiritual capital of India. Situated on the banks of the sacred Ganges river, it's a major pilgrimage center for Hindus.

Key Attractions

  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple
  • Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat
  • Sarnath (where Buddha gave his first sermon)
  • Banarasi Silk Sarees

Visitor Information

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Famous For: Spiritual experiences, Ghats, Silk textiles

Must Experience: Sunrise boat ride on Ganges
