The Royal Rajasthan

Jaipur is the capital of India's Rajasthan state, part of the Golden Triangle tourist circuit. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, the city is known for its vibrant culture, historic forts, and colorful bazaars.

Key Attractions

  • Amber Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar Observatory
